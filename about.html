About StrokePredict

StrokePredict is an innovative platform designed to help users assess their risk of stroke using advanced machine learning algorithms.

Our Mission

To provide accurate and accessible stroke risk assessment tools to help people take proactive steps towards better health.

Features

  • Structured data prediction based on health metrics
  • Medical image analysis for stroke detection
  • BMI calculator for health monitoring
  • Secure user accounts to track your health history

The Team

Our team consists of healthcare professionals and data scientists dedicated to improving stroke prevention through technology.
