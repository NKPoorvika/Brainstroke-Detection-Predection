Stroke Medications

Understanding and properly managing medications is crucial for stroke prevention and recovery.

Common Stroke Medications

These medications help prevent strokes or treat conditions that increase stroke risk

Antiplatelet Drugs

  • Aspirin
  • Clopidogrel (Plavix)
  • Dipyridamole (Persantine)

Prevent blood clots by keeping platelets from sticking together.

Anticoagulants

  • Warfarin (Coumadin)
  • Dabigatran (Pradaxa)
  • Apixaban (Eliquis)

Slow down blood clotting to prevent stroke in atrial fibrillation.

Blood Pressure Medications

  • ACE inhibitors
  • Beta blockers
  • Diuretics

Control high blood pressure, the leading cause of stroke.

Thrombolytics

  • Alteplase (tPA)

Emergency treatment to dissolve clots causing ischemic stroke.

Medication Management Tips

Consistency

Take medications at the same time each day to maintain steady levels in your body.

Refill Reminders

Set reminders to refill prescriptions before you run out.

Know Your Meds

Understand what each medication does and potential side effects.

Doctor Communication

Report any side effects or concerns to your healthcare provider.
