Life After Stroke

Recovery and rehabilitation resources for stroke survivors and their caregivers

Stroke Recovery Journey

Every stroke recovery is unique, but these stages provide a general framework

1

Acute Care

Immediate medical treatment to stabilize the patient and minimize brain damage.

2

Rehabilitation

Intensive therapy to regain lost abilities (physical, speech, occupational).

3

Community Reintegration

Adjusting to life at home and in the community with new abilities.

4

Long-Term Recovery

Ongoing progress and adaptation to lasting effects.

For Caregivers

Self-Care

Caregivers need to prioritize their own health to provide effective care.

Emotional Support

Be patient and understanding as recovery can be emotionally challenging.

Home Modifications

Adapt the living environment to accommodate new physical limitations.

Preventing Another Stroke

Medication Adherence

Take all prescribed medications as directed by your doctor.

Healthy Diet

Follow a heart-healthy diet low in sodium and saturated fats.

Regular Exercise

Engage in physical activity as approved by your healthcare team.

Regular Check-ups

Monitor blood pressure, cholesterol, and other risk factors.
